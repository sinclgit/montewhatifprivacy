# MonteWhatIf Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 13, 2026

MonteWhatIf is a retirement planning and Monte Carlo simulation app operated by SDot Solutions LLC. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, how it is stored, and what choices you have.

## Summary

MonteWhatIf is designed to run retirement simulations using information you enter into the app. The app does not require you to create an account, does not ask for financial account login credentials, and does not sell personal information.

## Information You Provide

MonteWhatIf may store the retirement planning inputs you enter, including:

- Current age and retirement age
- Current retirement savings
- Annual contribution amount
- Social Security estimate and start age
- Pension or other monthly income
- Retirement spending estimate
- Investment allocation preferences
- Simulation assumptions and scenario settings

This information is used only to calculate and display retirement simulation results inside the app.

## Local Storage

MonteWhatIf stores your profile and simulation settings locally on your device using device storage. This allows the app to remember your inputs between sessions.

MonteWhatIf does not operate a server for storing your retirement profile, and your retirement inputs are not intentionally transmitted to SDot Solutions LLC.

## Voice Features

MonteWhatIf includes voice command features for actions such as rerunning simulations and testing what-if scenarios.

When you use voice features:

- Your device microphone is used only after you grant permission.
- Speech recognition may be processed by Apple iOS speech recognition services or other system-level speech services provided by your device operating system.
- MonteWhatIf uses recognized text only to interpret commands inside the app.
- MonteWhatIf does not intentionally store audio recordings.
- MonteWhatIf does not intentionally transmit voice recordings to SDot Solutions LLC.

You can disable microphone or speech recognition permissions at any time in your device settings.

## App Store and TestFlight Data

If you install MonteWhatIf through TestFlight or the App Store, Apple may collect crash logs, diagnostics, installation data, and usage information according to Apple’s own privacy policies and developer tools.

SDot Solutions LLC may review Apple-provided crash reports or diagnostics to improve app reliability. These reports are managed by Apple and may include technical information such as device model, operating system version, crash details, and app version.

## Information We Do Not Collect

MonteWhatIf does not intentionally collect:

- Bank, brokerage, retirement account, or payroll login credentials
- Social Security numbers
- Tax identification numbers
- Precise location
- Contacts
- Photos or videos
- Health data
- Advertising identifiers for tracking

## Third-Party Services

MonteWhatIf may rely on platform services required for normal app operation, including Apple iOS speech recognition, TestFlight, App Store Connect, and crash/diagnostic reporting provided by Apple.

The app does not use third-party advertising networks and does not sell your personal information.

## Data Sharing

SDot Solutions LLC does not sell your personal information.

SDot Solutions LLC may share information only when necessary to:

- Comply with applicable law, regulation, or legal process
- Protect the rights, safety, or security of users, the app, or SDot Solutions LLC
- Diagnose and fix app crashes or technical problems using Apple-provided developer tools

## Data Retention and Deletion

Because MonteWhatIf stores retirement inputs locally on your device, you can remove locally stored app data by deleting the app from your device.

If future versions of MonteWhatIf add account-based cloud storage, this Privacy Policy will be updated before that functionality is made available.

## Children’s Privacy

MonteWhatIf is intended for retirement planning and is not directed to children under 13. SDot Solutions LLC does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Financial Disclaimer

MonteWhatIf provides educational retirement simulations and planning estimates. It does not provide financial, investment, tax, legal, or accounting advice. Simulation results are hypothetical and do not guarantee future outcomes.

## Changes to This Policy

SDot Solutions LLC may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the Effective Date above will be updated. Continued use of MonteWhatIf after changes means you accept the updated policy.
